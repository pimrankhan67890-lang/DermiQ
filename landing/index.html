How it works

Four steps to skin clarity

01
📸
Upload your photo

Drag and drop or tap to browse. We run a quality check first — brightness, blur, skin detection — so you never get garbage results from a bad photo.

02
🧠
AI analyses quickly

DermIQ scans your photo and returns top possibilities with confidence scores (educational only). Lighting and focus can change results.

03
📈
Guidance & products

You get safe general guidance and matched product links. You can optionally track what you use and how symptoms change over time.

04
🏥
Consult when needed

If you report red-flag symptoms, high severity, or you’re worried, DermIQ will recommend consulting a licensed clinician promptly.
