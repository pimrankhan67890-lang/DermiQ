Privacy Policy

DermIQ provides a non-diagnostic demo experience. This policy explains what we collect and how we handle uploaded images.

What we collect
Uploaded images: If you use the public analyzer, your image is sent to our backend service to generate results.
Technical logs: Servers may log basic request metadata (time, IP address, user agent) for security and reliability.
How we use it
We use uploads only to produce your result (top label, confidence, advice, and product suggestions). We do not sell personal data.
Retention
By default, images should not be stored long-term. If you deploy DermIQ publicly, set a clear retention period and update this page.
Your choices
Don’t upload images you consider sensitive. Prefer well-lit, non-identifying skin photos (avoid faces) when possible.
