Safety

Medical Disclaimer

DermIQ is a non-diagnostic educational tool. It does not replace professional medical advice.

Important
If you have severe pain, swelling, fever, spreading rash, rapid changes, bleeding, or you are worried, seek a licensed clinician promptly.
Limitations
Results can be affected by lighting, camera quality, skin tone variation, makeup, and image angle. A photo alone is not enough to diagnose a condition.
Products
Product suggestions are generic and may not be suitable for you. Patch test new products and stop use if irritation occurs.
